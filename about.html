Meet Kate

Learn more about Kate Rutledge.

Who is Kate Rutledge?

Kate is the postpartum doula at Milk Moon, and a 3-time mom herself. As a trained counselor with Nursing Mother's Counsel, she has in-depth experience counseling families with complex breastfeeding situations, as well as over seven years of her own breastfeeding adventures.

You can contact Kate to set up a meeting, or just chat, to see if she and your family are a fit.

What is a postpartum doula?

A postpartum doula is a woman who comes to your home and helps during the transition after a new baby (or babies!) arrives. She is there to answer questions, ease concerns, and aid with basic household, family, and infant care.

What is a Milk Moon?

According to the Old Farmer's Almanac, the Full Milk Moon rises in the month of May when cows, goats, and sheep enjoy sprouting weeds, grasses, and herbs in the pastures and produce lots of rich milk, full of vitamins.
