Services offered

Here is a partial list of the services offered by Kate. If you are looking for something specific that isn't here, please let her know when you chat.

Infant care & education

  • Feeding patterns and behaviors
  • Diaper changing and consultation (Got questions about disposables vs cloth? Kate's got answers.)
  • Sleep advice
  • Baby-wearing consultations
  • Baby-led bottle feeding

Maternal care

  • Breastfeeding help and support
  • Extensive training and experience in supporting difficult breastfeeding situations, such as:
    • Tongue tie
    • Flat or inverted nipples
    • Pumping
    • Feeding twins
    • Raynaud's phenomenom
  • Herbal supplement recommendations
  • Referrals to medical professionals in the area

Family care

  • Infant care oversight so parents can rest
  • Cooking and meal preparation
  • Older sibling care
  • Pet chores and dog walking
  • Errands - suddenly ran out of diapers? Send out the doula!
